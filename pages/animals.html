Tabby Cat

This tabby cat is calm and enjoys lounging in warm sunny spots. It likes gentle attention and would do well in a relaxed household that gives it space but also affection.

Snowball

Snowball is a white fluffy puppy that loves playtime and attention. It is energetic, friendly, and enjoys exploring new places. Snowball would do great with an active owner or a family with space to play.

Mittens

Mittens is a small, cute cat that loves to be near people. It enjoys light playtime but spends most of the day relaxing. Mittens would match well with someone who likes a low-maintenance but loving pet.
